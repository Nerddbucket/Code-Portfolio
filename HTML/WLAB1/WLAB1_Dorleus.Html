History

Atlanta is the capital city of Georgia and a major cultural and economic hub in the southeastern United States.

The city is also famous for its vibrant arts scene, world-class sports teams, and bustling neighborhoods filled with restaurants, music, and entertainment.

the city played a pivotal role in the Civil Rights Movement and is home to the Martin Luther King Jr. National Historical Park.

Atlanta boasts a diverse economy, with strong industries in aviation, film, technology, and finance.
